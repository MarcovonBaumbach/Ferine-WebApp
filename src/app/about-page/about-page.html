Forged in Fire, Fueled by Desire

Ferine started back in 2018 with one simple goal: to make music that comes from a burning soul and feels alive. We play alternative rock, but you’ll hear hints of grunge, punk, and hard rock in everything we do.

You'll experience our songs raw, loud and full of emotion, but also threaded with softer, melodic passages, carrying smoother, atmospheric sections — only to surge back into raw, driving intensity. Ferine is about raw feeling, relentless drive, and songs that stick with you long after the lights fade out.
